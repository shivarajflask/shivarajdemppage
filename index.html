What is the Hernia Mesh Lawsuit?

Hundreds of thousands of patients undergo hernia surgery in the U.S. each year. Hernias develop when part of an organ that is typically contained within the abdominal cavity protrudes through weakened muscles and tissues that line the abdomen. Hernia mesh devices were designed to provide an effective way to strengthen these weak spots and aid in healing..

Medical device companies that manufacture these devices have widely represented these products to be safe for use. Despite these claims, numerous patients have reported significant complications with their hernia mesh implants. Patients have experienced unnecessary physical pain, mental anguish, and financial expenses for corrective medical procedures.

When you contact our law firm, we put our extensive experience and proven results to work, holding medical device manufacturers accountable for your suffering.

What is Hernia Mesh?

Hernia mesh is used in surgical procedures to provide strengthening and support for tissues and organs. Some brands of hernia mesh, also referred to as surgical mesh, have been linked to serious medical complications after surgery.
Doctors rely on surgical mesh to repair hernias, pelvic organ prolapse, and stress urinary incontinence. Many recover from surgery without complications or further medical issues. However, some brands of surgical mesh have resulted in an increased risk of post-operation complications.
Defective surgical mesh may result in additional surgeries or procedures. Some patients report needing additional treatment to fight infection, or to repair perforation or adhesion.
